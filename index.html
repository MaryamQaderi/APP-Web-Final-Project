ROSE is a compilation of lively dining experiences.
Rose celebrates the best of Italian American dishes with welcoming hospitality and service.
Seasonal changes guide the menu of house-made pasta, antipasti, pizzas and more. Staying true to Afghanistan
dining traditions, Rose also offers a “For the Table”
chef-curated option for a family-style experience, offered nightly alongside the full à la carte menu.

Food

We are focused on showcasing the very best produce, cooked and prepared with the least intervention. We aim to create dishes that find harmony between simplicity and deliciousness. We are led by seasonality, and by cooking with and over fire, where the processes of wood-roasting, charring and slow cooking ensure that the spirit of our menu remains relaxed and timeless.

Drink

We have a contemporary list of low-intervention, organic, biodynamic and natural wines. Many of these are from recognisable appellations. Some are from lesser-known regions known for their high-quality production. And we carry a handful of the curious and unusual too. Our cocktail list focuses on seasonality with any fuss kept to a minimum.
